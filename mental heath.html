Causes of Depression and Anxiety

Depression and anxiety are two of the most common mental illnesses. The causes of depression and anxiety are complex and not fully understood. However, there are a number of factors that can contribute to these illnesses, including genetics, brain chemistry, life experiences, and medical conditions.

If you are experiencing symptoms of depression or anxiety, it is important to seek professional help. A therapist can help you to understand your condition and develop a treatment plan that is right for you.
